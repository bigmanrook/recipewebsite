Pizza is a very well-known food item across the globe. It is a great source of nutrition for most people on any diet, and is generally simple to make. Ingredients are as follows:

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato paste
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Vegetable or meat topic

    • Steps

    1. Bake your pizza base for 10 minutes
    2. Apply tomato paste to the pizza
    3. Add cheese and if topics are meant to be heated, add as well
    4. Bake your pizza for 15-20 minutes
    5. Your pizza is ready! Enjoy
